Overview

Smart Living Home & Garden is an online retailer specializing in outdoor decor. I worked as their head of all things print and web design.

I've personally overseen the design and development of their online store as it moved between different e-commerce platforms.

The website has been carefully curated from it's humble beginnings as a neatly crafted PHP application, with a single six mile tall CSS file. Later weathering the wild-west era of warring javascript frameworks, eventually moving to it's existing form as a well optimized, accessable, and responsive e-commerce mainstay.
